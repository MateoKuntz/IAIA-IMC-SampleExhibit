## About the Collection

This site provides a sampling of the historical materials in the fictional Institute of American Imperial Art's Imperial Movement Collection. The collection at large serves to maintain, preserve, and provide access to materials related to the United States of America's imperialist campaigns during the late 19th and early 20th centuries, most prominently referred to as the imperial movement.
